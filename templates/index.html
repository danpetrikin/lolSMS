Instructions:

1) Register for an account

2) Go to your email and click the activation link

3) Enter in your twilio Account SID and Auth Token, then click update. Note this will change the sms url for all your twilio numbers.

4) Create a new keyword, or just text into a number you own in that account and it will be created automatically.

5) You can add phone numbers manually, or they will be automatically added when they text in the key word

6) Select a group, enter a message, and click send. It will send that message to everyone in the group

The only browser I support for now is Google Chrome. It will likely work in others, but upgrade to Chrome :-p
